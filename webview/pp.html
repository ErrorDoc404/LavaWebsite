Privacy Policy for LavaCod

Last Updated: 03 June 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LavaCod ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Server Member Information

  • Usernames and Discriminators: We collect your username and discriminator to identify you within the server and to provide personalized interactions.
  • User IDs: We collect user IDs to uniquely identify users and manage interactions.
  • Roles and Permissions: We collect information about your roles and permissions within the server to ensure that the bot operates correctly and securely.

1.2. Message Content

  • Commands: We collect the content of messages that are commands directed at LavaCod to process those commands.
  • Deletion of Commands: We delete command messages after processing them to maintain a clean chat environment.

1.3. Voice Channel Activity

  • Voice Channel Connections: We collect information about your connections to voice channels to play music as requested.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. Providing Services

We use your information to operate LavaCod and provide its functionalities, including processing music commands, managing interactions, and playing music in voice channels.

2.2. Improving Services

We may use aggregated and anonymized data to improve the performance and features of LavaCod.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1. Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to operate LavaCod or as required by law.

3.2. Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of our users or others.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide LavaCod's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Command messages are deleted immediately after processing.

6. Your Rights

6.1. Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. You can do this by contacting us at admin@kartadharta.xyz.

6.2. Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data by contacting us. We will delete your data unless we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@kartadharta.xyz.

By using LavaCod, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
