Uninterrupted musical bliss awaits! Experience seamless playback in voice channels with our bot. Enjoy your favorite tunes without the hassle of lag, ensuring a smooth and immersive audio journey.

Server Settings

To set up LavaCod in your server, simply use the /setup [channel_name] command. This command will configure the bot to operate within the specified channel, which should be dedicated to music interactions. Once set up, you can enjoy endless music in your server seamlessly.
